Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an invitation?

Click "Start Creating" to open the editor. Choose or customize a template, fill in your details, and preview in real-time. It's that simple!

Can I download my invitation?

Yes! Click the "Download" button to save your invitation as a high-quality PNG image that you can print or share digitally.

Is InviteCraft really free?

Absolutely! InviteCraft is completely free to use. No hidden charges, no premium plans, no credit card required.

Can I upload my own images?

Yes! Upload custom images, drag them to reposition, and resize them to fit your invitation design perfectly.

How do I share my invitation?

Use our built-in sharing buttons for WhatsApp, Facebook, and Telegram, or copy the link to share anywhere.

Can I use InviteCraft on mobile?

Yes! InviteCraft is fully mobile-responsive. You can create invitations on your phone or tablet.

What file format is the download?

Invitations are downloaded as PNG files, which are compatible with all devices and can be easily printed.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed! InviteCraft works without registration. Start creating immediately.

Can I edit my invitation later?

Yes! Your invitation is saved in your browser. You can come back and edit it anytime during your session.

What happens to my data?

Your invitations are created and stored locally on your device. We never store your data on our servers.
